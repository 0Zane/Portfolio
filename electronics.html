Me and electronics
I started doing electronics in november 2025 and imediately fell in love with ESP32 s. I first made a room monitor to explore the usage of modules and quickly realized that there is much more than prototyping on breadboards and coding on Arduino IDE, so I started doing bigger projects and study electrcity laws to prepare myself for the world of engineering. In this section i will describe my projects from the hardware to the firmware.
SMART WATCH
Ths project if my first real project in electronic, i want to create everything , from the firmware, to the PCb to the case. After using the M5stickplus2 and lilygo T-Embed a lot , i discovered the world of embedded systems, IOT and cybergadgets. Most of the smart watches are capable of classic features like tracking your sleep , showing you your last notifications, tack the distance you walked in 1 day. but at the end ,all of them are the same and none of them stands out with unique features. Thats is why i decided to create this samrt watch from scratch that will be useful , useful for nerds but also for everyone. By adding special modules like IR receivers and transmitters or built in antennas with network scanning features, annoying tasks becomes funny rituals . Imagine being able to control the LEDS of you room with the watch at your wrist !
